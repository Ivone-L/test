All things knitting

Hello!

This is a site for people who love knitting and all things wool-related. You will find tutorials, reviews and different resources to help with your projects or find inspiration for your next project!

This is also a place where you can share your projects or ideas with others, ask questions or just hang out.

About me

I first started this page as a way to keep track of the different kinds of wool I've been using for various projects, and it just grew. As I started using different techniques for various things I thought it would be a great idea to also share this information with people who might find this useful!
